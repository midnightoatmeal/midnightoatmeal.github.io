A Message for Future Generations

Only the artists and philosophers, and now the scientists and mathematicians, are truly alive and aware of the sheer absurdity of existence, inventing new logical answers to make sense of it all. They have always shaped the public's opinions. The rest of the people can call them crazy, but all of their current thoughts that they pride themselves for being 'original' and 'normal' are really of the thinkers that came before them. The state of paralysing uncertainty is the price for being truly alive. And it is this very uncomfortable feeling that moves all the thinkers to go on to make better narratives for the masses; earlier it was religions, then it was philosophy, which grew out of it, and finally, now it's the science. Once we truly understand the thoughts of a genius, say someone like Einstein or Nietzsche, that understanding becomes the base level of new intelligence of our species. There was only one of them before, but because of books and other media, now whoever understands them, are practically Einstein and Nietzsche themselves, and they ought to build upon their theories and create even more creative and profound ones that can match with the unendingly complex universe accurately. Now there are millions of Einsteins and Nietzsches. And one of them would go on to make something even more bizarre and true. And on top of that, that same species is building machines that will gather all of the human knowledge and data, and uncover, simultaneously with us, the secrets of life and the universe. In a few decades, we are going to comprehend the most mind-splitting truths about the universe we find ourselves in, we are going to get our hands on the most powerful technologies, we'll conquer death, and colonize the galaxies. And that's just the beginning. We ought to throw away our TikToks, antidepressants, and all the things that help us escape and ignore the haunting question that's been staring at us for eons: why are you here and what's the purpose of everything? We need to stare back at that question courageously with hope, and work together, participate in society and life, collaborate to discover these secrets, solve these problems of society, to sustain the treaties and peace we have created, however flawed it may be, and make our experience in this mysterious universe even more fulfilling and enriching. Godspeed!
